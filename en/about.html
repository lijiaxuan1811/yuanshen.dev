About me...

I am a high-school student in Canada, and I wish to share my joy with ACG, amateur radio and retro computing with you.
My frequent played games are most slow-paced ones: Minecraft, Genshin Impact, Euro Truck Simulator 2 and Transport Fever 2.
I was continuously learning on computer hardware ever since 2017, and now I could even build my own computers pretty well.
I am also a new HAM in amateur radio since April 2024, with my call signs being VA3LPZ and BI4LPZ. If you are also a HAM, feel free to get in touch with me, VY 73!
If you are not one, welcome to join this big family of more than 3 million people in the world! Amateur radio community is a very friendly one,
and it is an activity involving multiple subjects. Joining the amateur radio society is also a great way to make friends from all over the world!
